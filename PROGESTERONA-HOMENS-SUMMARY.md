## Diagnostic Guidelines Included

### Elevated Progesterone (>1.0 ng/mL)
**Workup:**
```
17-OHP → Cortisol → ACTH → Androstenedione → DHEA-S → Testosterone
```

**Interpretation:**
- 17-OHP >10,000 ng/dL → Classic CAH
- 17-OHP 2,000-10,000 ng/dL → Non-classic CAH
- 17-OHP 200-1,000 ng/dL → ACTH stim test needed

**Treatment (if CAH):**
- Hydrocortisone 15-25 mg/day OR Prednisone 5-7.5 mg/day
- Monitor: 17-OHP, androstenedione, testosterone

### Metabolic Screening
If progesterone >1.4 ng/mL:
- Fasting glucose
- HbA1c
- OGTT
