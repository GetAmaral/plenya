## Key Scientific Evidence

### Manganese Neurotoxicity Mechanisms

1. **Oxidative Stress:** Mn accumulation generates reactive oxygen species (ROS) overwhelming cellular antioxidant defenses
2. **Mitochondrial Dysfunction:** Direct interaction with Complex I of respiratory chain
3. **Glutamatergic Excitotoxicity:** Impaired glutamate metabolism and excitatory neurotransmission
4. **Neuroinflammation:** Microglial activation and inflammatory cascade
5. **Dopaminergic Neurodegeneration:** Selective damage to substantia nigra pars compacta

### Novel Therapeutic Insights (2023)

**Biotin Neuroprotection:**
- Demonstrated in Drosophila models and human iPSC-derived neurons
- Mechanisms: mitochondrial modulation, reduced oxidative stress
- Dosage: 300mg/day shows promise
- Clinical trials needed for human efficacy confirmation

### Exposure Thresholds (EFSA 2023)

- **Dietary UL:** 8 mg/day for adults
- **Occupational Concern:** <0.2 mg/m³ air already shows effects on brain function and motor skills
- **At-Risk Populations:** Chronic liver disease, iron deficiency anemia
